Detailed Description

Represents a Tangle Cube primitive for raytracing.

The TangleCube class defines an implicit surface known as a Tangle Cube, which is characterized by its center point and size. The surface is defined by the equation: x⁴ - 5x² + y⁴ - 5y² + z⁴ - 5z² + 11.8 = 0. This creates a cube-like shape with rounded edges and corners.
